About Indian.SG

Our Vision & Purpose

At Indian.SG, we stand as a symbol of cultural exchange and unity. We bring people together by sharing the beauty of Tamil language and Indian culture. Through fun and creative events, we help different communities in Singapore connect, understand each other better, and celebrate together with joy and pride.

Our Ambition

We hope to bring people of all ages together through stories, language, and traditions. Our goal is to create a friendly and welcoming space where children, youth, parents, and grandparents feel excited to learn Tamil, stay connected to their roots, and play a part in Singapore’s multicultural story.

How We Do It

We work with teachers, volunteers, and community groups to run fun and meaningful activities — like storytelling, youth festivals, language classes, and cultural events. Our goal is to make learning Tamil and Indian culture enjoyable, creative, and easy for everyone to join.
