Our Vision & Purpose

At Indian.SG, we believe in nurturing a society where the Tamil language and Indian heritage are not only preserved, but thrive through the creative spirit and collective pride of the community.

Our Ambition

We aim to be a catalyst for cultural continuity — bringing generations together through shared stories, language, and traditions. Our ambition is to create a vibrant and inclusive space where every child, youth, parent, and elder feels inspired to engage with Tamil, connect with their roots, and contribute meaningfully to Singapore’s multicultural tapestry.

How We Do It

We partner with educators, volunteers, and organizations to deliver initiatives such as storytelling sessions, youth-focused festivals, language enrichment programs, and community events. Our approach is grounded in creativity, relevance, and fun — ensuring that cultural learning is always engaging and accessible.

Join us in shaping a future where Tamil is celebrated not just at home, but in the hearts of all who call Singapore home.
