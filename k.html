Study Guide

Readable guide for students: key ideas, mistakes, exam tips.

Key Concepts
  • LAN: local network within a small area (lab, classroom).
  • WAN: network across large distances (campuses/cities) — often operated by ISPs.
  • Switch vs Router: switch connects within a LAN; router connects networks together.
Common mistakes
  • Mixing up “modem” and “router”.
  • Thinking Wi-Fi is the same as the Internet.
