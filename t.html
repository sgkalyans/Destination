About Us

Indian.SG is a community-centric organisation that celebrates and promotes Tamil and Indian culture in Singapore. Through storytelling, language workshops, music, arts, and cultural events, we engage families, children, and educators in meaningful experiences that preserve and showcase our heritage.

What We Do

Storytelling

Engaging Tamil storytelling sessions for children using puppets, music, and visuals to inspire language and imagination.

Workshops

Interactive language and culture workshops designed for preschoolers, youths, and families to learn Tamil in a fun way.

Cultural Showcases

Opportunities for children to express their talents through folk songs, drama, and Tamil language performances in a joyful and encouraging setting.

Cultural Events

Special events during the Tamil Language Festival and other occasions to celebrate our heritage and community spirit.
