About Us

Indian.SG is a community-centric organisation that celebrates and promotes Tamil and Indian culture in Singapore. Through storytelling, cultural workshops, music, arts, and festive events, we engage families, children, and educators in meaningful experiences that preserve and showcase our heritage.

What We Do

Storytelling

Engaging Tamil storytelling sessions for children using puppets, music, and visuals to inspire language and imagination.

Cultural Workshops

Interactive cultural workshops designed for preschoolers, youths, and families to explore Indian traditions, arts, and values in engaging and creative ways.

Cultural Showcases

Opportunities for children to express their talents through folk songs, drama, and Tamil language performances in a joyful and encouraging setting.

Integration & Community Engagements

As part of Racial Harmony Day and National Day celebrations, we engage young participants through activities like cultural dress showcases, creative art expression, and Tamil speech sharing—nurturing pride in identity while fostering appreciation for Singapore’s rich multicultural fabric.

Cultural Celebrations

We participate in special occasions such as the Tamil Language Festival, Tamil Youth Festival, Writers Festival, and other meaningful events—celebrating our heritage and strengthening community spirit through creative and cultural expressions.
